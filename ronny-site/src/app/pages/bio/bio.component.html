Biography

Ronny Bazan is an assistant teaching professor for the IT Program in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department at the University of Missouri. He was the recipient of a Fulbright scholarship to obtain his MS in Computer Science at MU where he was awarded as an Outstanding Master Student. He went on to earn a PhD in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the University of Missouri. Before joining MU, he worked as a senior system administrator in industry. During that time he obtained training in Advanced Windows Servers administration in Japan and Computer Networking and Advanced Linux Servers administration in India through long-term scholarships. He has obtained professional certifications from Microsoft, Cisco and VMware.
